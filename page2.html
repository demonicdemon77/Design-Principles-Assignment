Examples of Design Principles

  1. Contrast
    • Apple
    • Makes use of bold text, imagery, and white space.
  2. Repetition
    • Airbnb
    • Uses similar colors and layouts throughout the site.
  3. Alignment
    • Stripe
    • Maintains consistent organization across pages.
  4. Proximity
    • Notion
    • Groups related information together.

Good web design helps users understand information quickly.

Using these four principles makes websites easier to read and navigate.
